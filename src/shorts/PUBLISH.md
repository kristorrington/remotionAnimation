# Shorts publishing copy (YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Reels)

One entry per `ShortSpec` — written when the spec is added (see README, "The
pattern"). Copy-paste at upload time. Rules used here:

- **Title:** ≤ 60 chars before truncation matters; front-load the hook words;
  no clickbait the video doesn't pay off; end with `#Shorts`.
- **Description:** line 1 restates the hook (it's indexed + sometimes shown),
  2–3 lines of what the viewer learns, a comment-bait question, the follow CTA,
  then 4–6 hashtags. Never invent claims the video doesn't make.
